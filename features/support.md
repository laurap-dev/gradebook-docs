# Customer Support

The Customer Support feature provides access to help resources, subscription management, and direct contact with the GradeBook support team.

## Requirements

- **Valid License**: ❌ Not required (always accessible)
- **Must be GradeBook**: ❌ Not required (works on any spreadsheet)
- **Premium**: ❌ Not required

{: .note }
> Support is available to all users, regardless of license or subscription status.

## Accessing the Feature

1. Open Google Sheets (any spreadsheet)
2. Click **Extensions → GradeBook → Support → Customer Support & Subscription Details**
3. The support sidebar will open

## Subscription Management

### View Subscription Details

The sidebar displays:
- **License Status**: Active, Expired, or Trial
- **License Type**: Free, Standard, or Premium
- **Expiration Date**: When current license expires
- **Purchase Date**: When license was obtained
- **License Key**: Your unique identifier

### Managing Your License

**View Purchase History**
- See all past transactions
- Download receipts
- Track renewal dates

**Update License**
- Enter new license key
- Activate after purchase
- Extend expiration date

**Upgrade to Premium**
- Compare feature differences
- Purchase premium subscription
- Immediate activation

### Subscription Renewal

**Auto-Renewal** (if available)
- Automatic subscription renewal
- Charged to payment method on file
- Email reminders before renewal

**Manual Renewal**
- Purchase new license period
- Enter new key when received
- Seamless continuation

**Renewal Reminders**
- 30 days before expiration
- 7 days before expiration
- Day of expiration
- Grace period after expiration (if applicable)

{: .important }
> Some features may be disabled after license expiration. Renew promptly to avoid interruption.

## Account Management

### License Transfer

Transfer license to different Google account:
1. Contact support with request
2. Provide both account emails
3. Verify ownership
4. Transfer processed within 24 hours

### Refund Policy

- Review marketplace refund terms
- Contact support for assistance
- Typically 30-day window
- Conditions may apply

### Cancel Subscription

To cancel auto-renewal:
1. Access subscription management
2. Click "Cancel Auto-Renewal"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. License valid until expiration date

{: .note }
> Canceling auto-renewal doesn't immediately revoke access. You retain access until the current period ends.

## Privacy and Data

### What Data is Collected

GradeBook collects:
- License information
- Usage statistics (anonymized)
- Error reports
- Support interactions

**Not collected:**
- Student grades
- Personal student information
- Gradebook content

### Data Security

- All data encrypted in transit
- Stored on Google's secure servers
- Access limited to authorized personnel
- Regular security audits

### Data Deletion

To delete your data:
1. Contact support with request
2. Specify what to delete
3. Confirmation provided
4. Deletion completed within 30 days

## Troubleshooting Support Access

### Issue: Support menu doesn't appear

**Solutions:**
- Refresh Google Sheets
- Reinstall GradeBook add-on
- Check Extensions menu
- Clear browser cache

### Issue: Can't see subscription details

**Solutions:**
- Check internet connection
- Verify Google account
- May take 24 hours after purchase
- Contact support if persists

### Issue: License key not working

**Solutions:**
- Copy/paste carefully (no spaces)
- Verify key hasn't expired
- Check it's for correct product
- Contact support with purchase details
