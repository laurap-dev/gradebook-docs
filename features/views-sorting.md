## Tips

- **Focus on what matters**: Use "Hide Empty" options to reduce clutter when you have many assignments or students
- **Quick grading**: Sort students by grade (lowest first) to quickly identify who needs extra help
- **Find recent work**: Sort assignments by date (newest first) to see your latest evaluations first
