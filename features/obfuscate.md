# Send Obfuscated GradeBook

Send an obfuscated copy of your GradeBook to customer support for assistance. This creates a secure version where all student information is automatically replaced with encrypted values.

## Requirements

- **Valid License**: ✅ Required
- **Must be GradeBook**: ✅ Required
- **Premium**: ❌ Not required

## Accessing the Feature

1. Open your GradeBook spreadsheet
2. Click **Extensions → GradeBook → Support → Send Obfuscated GradeBook**
3. The Send Obfuscated GradeBook sidebar will open

---

## The Send Obfuscated GradeBook Menu

### Privacy Protection

Your student data is completely protected. GradeBook automatically:

- Creates a secure copy on your Google Drive
- Replaces all student data with irreversible encrypted values
- Removes revision history for additional security
- Ensures no sensitive information can be viewed by support

### Describe Your Issue

Enter a description of the problem you're experiencing. This helps the support team understand what to look for in your obfuscated GradeBook.

| Field | What to Include |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Issue Description** | Describe the problem you're experiencing. Include what you expected to happen vs. what actually happened. |

| Button | What It Does |
|--------|--------------|
| **Send to Support Team** | Creates the obfuscated copy and sends it along with your issue description to the support team. You'll receive a response via email. |

{: .note }
> The Send button is disabled until you enter an issue description.

---

## Tips

- **Be specific**: The more detail you provide about the problem, the faster support can help you
- **Your data stays private**: Support never sees real student names, emails, or personal information
- **Check your email**: You'll receive a support response at the email address associated with your account
