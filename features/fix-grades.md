## When to Use Fix Grades

Use this feature when:
- Overall grades aren't calculating correctly
- You see formula errors in grade columns
- Grades stopped updating when you enter new scores
- You accidentally edited a formula cell

---

## Tips

- **Try Quick Fix first** - It's faster and often solves the problem
- **Use Complete Fix** if Quick Fix doesn't work, or if you notice issues across multiple columns
- Your actual grade data (the scores you entered) is not affectedâ€”only the calculation formulas are repaired
